Dobok (Uniform)


    A "Dobok" is a traditional uniform used by practitioners of the Taekwondo Martial Art.


  • It is comprised of the following:
    1. The Jacket
    2. The Pants
    3. The Belt

Poomsae


    A "Poomsae" is a sequence of movements with a specific form and motion that is practiced individually.


  • There are a total of 17, the first 8 are required to become a 1st degree blackbelt.

  • The names of the first 8 are:
    1. Taegeuk Il Jang
    2. Taegeuk I Jang
    3. Taegeuk Sam Jang
    4. Taegeuk Sa Jang
    5. Taegeuk Oh Jang
    6. Taegeuk Yuk Jang
    7. Taegeuk Chil Jang
    8. Taegeuk Pal Jang

  • These techniques teach structured fundamental principles, discipline and coordination.
